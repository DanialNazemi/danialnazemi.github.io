Introduction

Hi, I'm Danial Nazemi — a Product, UI/UX designer and voice actor who believes great design lives at the intersection of beauty and function. I craft interfaces that don't just look good, but feel inevitable to use — and voices that don't just sound good, but feel impossible to forget.

With a background in both visual design and human psychology, I approach every project with deep empathy for the end user and relentless attention to detail — whether that's a pixel-perfect interface or a character brought to life through sound.

When I'm not pushing pixels or stepping into a recording booth, you'll find me exploring nature trails, learning, or diving into the games.
